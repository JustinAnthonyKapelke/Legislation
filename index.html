01 / 6 Non-Negotiable Requirements
01
🎯

Security by Design

  • Plan threats before writing a single line
  • Threat modeling during the design phase
  • Security cannot be bolted on afterward
  • All decisions must be documented
02
🪲

Vulnerability Management

  • Active exploit → 24h early warning
  • Full report required within 72 hours
  • "Fix it in the next sprint" = illegal
  • Coordinated disclosure process required
03
🔐

Secure by Default

  • Products must ship secure out of the box
  • Zero weak or default passwords
  • All security features on by default
  • No manual hardening required from user
04
⛓️

Dependency Management

  • SBOM required for every dependency
  • Automated scanning for known risks
  • Log4j-style incidents are exactly what CRA targets
  • Know your full supply chain
05
🧪

Risk Assessment

  • Formal threat identification is mandatory
  • Conducted during design and development
  • Document all mitigations taken
  • Risk management is an iterative process
06
📝

Technical Documentation

  • Audit-ready evidence at all times
  • Design decisions must be logged
  • Maintained risk register required
  • CE marking declaration of conformity
