6 Non-Negotiable Requirements

🎯

Security by Design

  • Plan threats before coding
  • Threat modeling during design
  • Not bolted on after
  • Documented decisions
🪲

Vulnerability Management

  • Find & fix vulnerabilities
  • 24h early warning required
  • 72h full report required
  • "Fix it later" = illegal
🔐

Secure by Default

  • Ship secure out of the box
  • No weak default passwords
  • All security enabled
  • No manual hardening
⛓️

Dependency Management

  • SBOM for every library
  • Auto-scan for risks
  • Log4j? CRA targets this
  • Know your supply chain
🧪

Risk Assessment

  • Identify threats formally
  • During design & development
  • Document mitigations
  • Risk management is iterative
📝

Technical Documentation

  • Audit-ready evidence
  • Design decisions logged
  • Risk register maintained
  • CE marking proof
